Rainbow lorakeets

The rainbow lorikeet is a medium-sized parrot. The head is dark green with a yellow-green neck, the rest of the upper part (wings, back and tail) is dark green. The chest is red with dark blue stripes. The abdomen is dark green, and the thighs and rump are yellow with dark green markings. In flight, the yellow wing bar contrasts sharply with the red underwing.
There is little clear distinction between the sexes; however, to a keen observer of their colors and behavior, their dimorphism is easily discernible.
